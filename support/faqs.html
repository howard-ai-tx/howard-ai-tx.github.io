What is Howard?

Howard is a locally hosted AI assistant that lives inside your home or office on dedicated hardware, not in a cloud data center somewhere. He has his own phone number. You call or text him, and he gets things done. Booking flights, managing your calendar, emailing clients, handling the day-to-day stuff that eats your time. He's not a chatbot. He's not a suggestion engine. He executes.

Howard handles real tasks like scheduling, communications, travel booking, reminders, administrative work, and more. The list grows as we expand his integrations. The short version: if it's something you'd normally delegate to a personal assistant, Howard can likely handle it.

Howard has his own dedicated phone number, email, and more points of contact. Text him, email him, or both, from anywhere. You're at the airport, you need something handled back home. You text Howard. Done.

Privacy & Security

Every one of those assistants sends your data to a corporate server the moment you interact with them. Howard runs entirely on hardware inside your building. Your conversations, your tasks, and your data never leaves your location. Ever. The privacy pitch is simple: don't trust it, unplug it.

Nowhere. It stays on the hardware installed in your home or office. Howard AI never sees it, never stores it remotely, and it never touches an outside server.

Howard has no cloud presence to attack. There is no remote server storing your information, which eliminates the most common attack vector entirely. Your hardware is local, your data is local, and your security is not dependent on a third party's data center staying safe.

Installation & Hardware

You place your order, we build your Howard unit, schedule an installation appointment, and one of our technicians comes to your location and handles everything. Ethernet connection, setup, testing, and a walkthrough so you know exactly how to use him. We don't ship a box and leave you with a manual.

Your monthly subscription includes technician dispatch at no additional charge. If something goes wrong with your hardware, we come to you. No shipping it somewhere, no waiting on hold with a call center. A real person shows up.

Yes. Once you've paid the upfront hardware cost, the unit is yours. The monthly subscription covers ongoing support, API updates, security checks, and technician access, but not the hardware itself.
