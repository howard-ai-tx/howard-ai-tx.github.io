About Support

Support that
physically shows up.

Howard AI support is built around local technicians, direct communication, and real infrastructure — not ticket queues or outsourced call centers.

Local Technicians

Every Howard installation and support visit is handled by Houston-based technicians trained directly by Howard AI.

Real Communication

Call or text our support line and a real person responds. If an issue requires a visit, we dispatch directly to your location.

Ongoing Maintenance

Your subscription includes software updates, security checks, hardware diagnostics, and technician dispatch at no additional charge.

Houston-first support.

Howard AI was designed around the belief that support should feel personal again. When something goes wrong, you should be able to reach a real person who understands your system and can physically help solve the problem.

Because Howard runs locally on dedicated hardware inside your home or office, support works differently. Your data remains yours. Your hardware stays local. And if you ever need help, we come to you.
