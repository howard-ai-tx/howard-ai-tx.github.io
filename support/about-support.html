Installation

White-Glove Setup

We don't ship Howard in a cardboard box for you to figure out. Every deployment includes on-site installation by a Houston-based technician to ensure your local network is secure and Howard is calibrated to your space.

From rack-mounting Pro units in your office to setting up Home units in your living room, we handle the cables so you can handle the intelligence.

Local Dispatch

If your hardware needs attention, we don't send you a shipping label. We send a person. Our technicians are based right here in Houston.

Network Health

We provide ongoing monitoring for your local GPU cluster to ensure Howard is running at peak efficiency without leaving your private network.

Hardware Warranty

All Howard Home and Pro units are covered by a comprehensive local hardware warranty. If a component fails, we replace it on-site.
