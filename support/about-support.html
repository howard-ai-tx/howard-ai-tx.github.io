About Support

Real support.
Real people.

Howard AI was built around local service. When something needs attention, a technician shows up. No ticket queues. No overseas call centers. No shipping hardware across the country.

The support network is the product.

Most technology companies optimize for distance. Howard AI optimizes for proximity.



We believe trust matters more than feature lists. That means local technicians, direct communication, and support that feels human instead of transactional.

Houston-Based

Howard AI currently serves the Greater Houston area with locally based technicians and support operations.

Technician Dispatch

If something goes wrong, a real person comes to your location as part of your monthly subscription.

Direct Communication

Call or text support directly. No ticket portals. No layers of outsourced support between you and a solution.

Built for homes and businesses.

Whether Howard is installed in a family home, a law office, a medical practice, or a growing business, the support experience stays the same: responsive, local, and personal.



The goal isn’t to become the biggest AI company. The goal is to become the most trusted.
