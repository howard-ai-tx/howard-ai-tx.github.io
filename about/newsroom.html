June 2026
HowardAI announces Howard Core — locally hosted AI for homes and small businesses.

HowardAI today announced Howard Core, the company's launch product and the first locally hosted AI assistant designed for residential and small business use. Howard Core ships as a self-install hardware unit and is accessible via a dedicated phone number. It executes real tasks — booking flights, managing calendars, handling email — without any data leaving the customer's property.

HowardAI website launches ahead of Howard Core pre-order opening.

The HowardAI website is now live at howardai.com. The site covers Howard Core product details, the company's privacy architecture, and how to get in touch ahead of pre-orders opening. No purchases are live yet — interested customers are invited to contact the team directly.
