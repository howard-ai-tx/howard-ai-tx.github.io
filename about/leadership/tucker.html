Executive Profile

Tucker Pate

Co-Founder

Tucker Pate is the Co-Founder of Howard AI and oversees the hardware and software development of all Howard models.

Since co-founding Howard AI, Tucker has been the driving force behind the company’s mission to bring high-performance, private AI into the home. He leads the engineering responsible for Howard’s vertically integrated stack, ensuring that proprietary hardware and software work in perfect unison. Under his leadership, Howard AI has developed a unique local-hosting architecture that allows for complex task execution, from travel booking to financial management without ever sending customer data to a cloud data center.

Prior to co-founding Howard AI, Tucker developed a deep expertise in technical operations and product architecture, shaped by a belief that the future of artificial intelligence must be anchored in local privacy and tangible service. His focus on "Assistant Intelligence" ensures that every Howard unit is built to order and maintained with the precision required for mission-critical administrative work. Tucker holds a degree from no university at the current moment.
