About Investors

Built for the long term.

HowardAI is pre-launch and pre-revenue. We're building carefully, not quickly, because the product requires it.

The opportunity

The AI assistant market is large and growing — but every major player has made the same architectural choice: the cloud. That choice makes the products faster to ship and easier to scale, and it exposes every user's data to infrastructure they don't control.

Howard takes the opposite position. Local hardware, no cloud dependency, privacy that isn't contingent on trusting a company's policy. That position is currently unoccupied at the consumer and small business level.

Howard Core is the first product. It ships at $2,999.99 with a monthly subscription covering updates, monitoring, support, and a priority replacement warranty. The roadmap includes Pro and Business configurations as the core product matures.

Pre-launch stage

Howard is pre-revenue and pre-launch. We are currently preparing Howard Core for its first customer shipments.

US market focus

Howard ships to households and small businesses across the United States. The initial launch is domestic only.

Hardware + subscription model

One-time hardware purchase at $2,999.99, recurring monthly subscription for updates, support, and warranty. Two revenue streams from day one.
