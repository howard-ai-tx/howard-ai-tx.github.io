Call Him. Text Him. Give Him Work.

Howard Home is a locally hosted AI assistant installed directly inside your home by Houston technicians.

Howard has his own dedicated phone number. Anyone in the household can call or text him from anywhere and assign real work.

He books flights, manages calendars, reviews bills, drafts emails, makes phone calls, and handles the administrative work that fills your day.
