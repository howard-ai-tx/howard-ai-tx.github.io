Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Howard AI is a technology company that ships locally hosted AI assistants to homes and businesses across the United States. Our entire product is built on a single architectural principle: your data stays where it belongs — inside your building, not on our servers.

That same principle extends to how we run this website. We collect only what we need, we use it only for what we say, and we do not sell it. This policy explains what information we collect through howardai.us, how we use it, and what choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

When you fill out a contact form on our website, we collect:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address
  • Your reason for reaching out
  • The content of your message

We collect this information only when you voluntarily submit it. Visiting our website without filling out a form does not result in us collecting any personally identifiable information.

We may also collect basic technical information automatically when you visit the site, such as your browser type, device type, and general geographic region. This information is not linked to your identity.

As our website grows, we may add analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our content. If and when we do, we will update this policy accordingly and ensure any tools we use are configured to minimize data collection.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit through our contact form to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Follow up on questions or requests you have raised
  • Understand what prospective customers need

We do not use your contact form submission to add you to any marketing list without your explicit permission.

In the future, we plan to offer an email notification list for product launches, availability announcements, and company updates. If we add this feature, signing up will always be a separate, deliberate opt-in — not an automatic consequence of contacting us. You will be able to unsubscribe at any time.

3. How We Store Your Information

Contact form submissions are received and stored within our business communication tools. Access is limited to the HowardAI founding team. We do not operate data warehouses, advertising platforms, or third-party CRMs that would receive your information.

We retain contact form submissions for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain a record of the conversation. If you would like your information deleted, see Section 6.

4. Who We Share Your Information With

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with advertisers, data brokers, or third-party marketing platforms.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist us in operating the website or responding to inquiries, and only to the extent necessary for that purpose
  • If required by law, court order, or valid legal process
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of the company, in which case we would notify affected users in advance

Any service provider who receives your information is required to handle it with the same care we apply ourselves.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not currently use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

If we implement analytics tools in the future, we will update this policy and, where required, ask for your consent before placing non-essential cookies on your device. You can control cookie behavior through your browser settings at any time.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal information we hold about you
  • Request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Request that we delete your information
  • Withdraw any consent you have previously given

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address listed in Section 8. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently received information from a child, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how we handle your information, reach out to us directly:

HowardAI, LLC
The Woodlands, Texas
contact@howardai.us

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our website and business evolve. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We recommend checking back periodically. Continued use of the website after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
